About me

My name is Hamida Tania and I am a eleventh grader at Thomas A. Edison CTE Highs school. In our team my role is to work with my teammates to come up with solutions and ideas for our social issue app. I also help with designing. Our social issue are community issues such as poverty. We plan on solving our community issue by creating community service volunteer oppurtunities. This way we can solve community issues while high schoolers are also able to recieve volunteer oppurtunties. One of our features in our app is a way for users who are in need of clothes to request for donations. They are able to look at each piece of clothe as if it was an online store and even filter to their size. I understand that poverty is a complex issue and cannot be solved by a single solution. Our app seeks to provide a range of services to help people in need.We hope to inspire others to take action and join us in our efforts to make the world a better place!
